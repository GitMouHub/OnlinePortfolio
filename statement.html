I am a studio practice art student whose art is mainly traditional. I have just started to learn how to handle technological art tools as a way to expand and grow my art skills. Many of my artworks contain fantasy themes due to being interested in the genre when I was young. I had no way to do art online, so my only choice was to use either pencil or pen. As I started to draw more and more, I began to incorporate portions of my life experiences in my artworks(not directly; metaphorically).

I am an introverted person at heart; however, I do go outside to enjoy the breeze and looking at the clear, blue sky. As to why I stare at the sky so much is due to the fictional creature called a dragon. A large lizard-like beast with amazing scales and the ability to soar through the skies with its enormous wings. It gives a feeling of freedom when thinking of those things in tandem with each other. Though what I mostly draw look like humans, they are creatures that blend in with modern human society. I can only do so much with traditional drawing until I need to express my characters in their fullest colors, which is why I am learning to do art online.
